I made $5,000 last week from a simple weekly offer.
- No fancy funnel.
- No big launch.
- Just one clear outcome 
- Delivered in 7 days.
Here’s the backstory:
A friend of mine sells “Website in a Week.”
One outcome. One price. Done fast.
Somehow, he managed to do this using the templates he created.
I liked the idea, so I tried it with my service.
→ Brand, strategy, positioning.
- Cut the extra stuff
- Focused on one result
- Delivered in one week
Fast. Simple. Clean.
Profit is around $3.8k - Revenue is $5k
76% profit from full systemization.
Here’s how to build your own Weekly Offer:
1. Choose one outcome you already deliver
→ Like a sales page, brand doc, profile refresh, or pitch deck
2. Cut everything else
- Keep only what gets results
- Promise delivery in 5–7 days
3. Give it a clear name
- “Brand Sprint”
- “LinkedIn Makeover”
- “Website in a Week”
4. Price it right
- $1–$2.5K (Not too high, not too low)
- Fast and focused work = decent price
This is great when:
✔️ You want quick cash
✔️ You’re between projects
✔️ You want momentum without building a full offer
Don’t overthink it. Just ship it.
Try it once → you’ll want to run it again.
