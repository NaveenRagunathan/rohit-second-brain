You think the problem is your content. 👇
But here’s what’s actually killing your business:
1. You sell skills → but clients want money and clarity.
2. You overdeliver for bad clients → and attract more of them.
3. You let clients run your day → then wonder where time went.
4. You take unstructured calls → and wonder why they ghost.
5. You chase perfect → while others close messy $5K deals.
6. You redesign your site → because outreach feels scary.
7. You post daily → but never make anyone want to buy.
8. You tweak your portfolio → but never book a call.
9. You look premium → but charge like a beginner.
10. You care more about peers than paying clients.
11. You tweak logos → hoping it fixes positioning.
12. You never track → just guess what’s working.
13. You launch once → it fails, then you vanish.
14. You keep saying “not ready yet.”
15. You stay busy → but stuck.
If you work really hard on the wrong things,
no matter how hard you work, you’ll never get ahead.
Focus on the right things.
Work HARD on those.
Not just the things you like.
Not just what feels easy.
Most people only work on what they enjoy 
even when they know what actually needs fixing.
But they avoid it.
Because it’s hard.
Because it’s uncomfortable.
And this post is your reminder.
Refocus & Execute.
