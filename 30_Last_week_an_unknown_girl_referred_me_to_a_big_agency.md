Last week, an unknown girl referred me to a big agency.
(Didn’t know who she was until that day)
One of their Account Directors booked a call.
By the end we closed a 6-month retainer.
I asked him,
 “Where’d you hear about us?”
He said,
 “My friend. She really admires your work.
 Told me your agency is the way to go.”
On the call, I nodded like I knew her.
Truth is → I had no idea who she was. 😂
After the call, I opened my followers.
Scrolled.
Scrolled some more.
Finally
found her.
- She wasn’t a creator.
- She wasn’t a coach.
- She wasn’t a “personal brand.”
She was just a regular professional.
Quiet. No posts. Few reposts only.
But she had been watching me for a long time.
I texted her:
 “Thanks for referring me.”
She replied:
 “My pleasure. I’ve been following your work for a very long time.
 I really love the work.”
That one mention turned into a 6-month client.
That’s the power of showing your work.
That’s the magic of
putting your work out there.
You just never know
who’s watching.
Don’t be shy.
Show your work.
Do it.
