I was a designer when I started writing online.
Not a copywriter.
Writing was never my job, never my thing, and honestly? 
I was terrible at it.
Someone asked me recently how I learned.
The answer is embarrassingly simple.
I followed Jasmin Alić. 
Back then he had a page where he shared his best performing posts. 
I screenshotted every single one and rewrote them from scratch using my own experience and my own industry.
Thats how I used to do practice
Overtime I got good at it.
If you want to learn any new skill, the best thing you can do is find an expert and mimic him to practice
Thats all, don't watch endless tutorials on youtube.
P.S. I'll ask Jasmin Alić if that page is still live.
