The First $10K Felt Like Magic.
I thought I made it.
$10K from branding projects.
Finally, proof that this could work.
Then reality hit.
✗ Clients dictated timelines.
✗ Every project felt like a reset.
✗ I was busy, but never in control.
✗ No matter how hard I worked, I felt stuck.
I wasn’t running a business.
I was just another helping hand.
I was surviving.
That’s when I made the shift:
✔ From “designer” to brand strategist
✔ From one-off gigs to premium retainers
✔ From chasing clients to clients chasing me
✔ From delivering assets to driving outcomes
That first $10K felt good.
But the first $10K client?
That changed everything.
More respect. More control.
More time. More growth.
But most importantly?
More freedom.
If you feel overworked, underpaid,
and stuck in the cycle of endless projects.
It’s not about working harder.
It’s about changing your positioning.
Here’s the formula that worked for me:
1. Clarify your value 
→ Stop selling services, start selling outcomes.
2. Package your expertise
→ Shift from one-off projects to high-ticket offers.
3. Own your positioning 
→ Be the authority, so the right clients seek you.
4. The most important 'Ignore the noise'
→ Be selfishly focused on your path. Stop watching others. Their journey isn’t yours.
Hope it helps ♥️
Which of these do you need to work on?
