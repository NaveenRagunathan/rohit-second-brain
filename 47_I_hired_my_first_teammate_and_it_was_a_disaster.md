I hired my first teammate… and it was a disaster.
At first, it was just me.
No team. No backup. Just hustle.
I took on everything
Sales. Writing. Design. Delivery.
Late nights. No weekends. No breaks.
If I didn’t work, I didn’t earn.
Then came a project too big to handle.
I had two choices:
❌ Say no and stay small.
✅ Take the leap and grow.
So, I hired my first teammate.
And they messed up.
Missed deadlines. Wrong designs.
I had to redo everything myself.
A waste of time and money.
For a moment, I thought:
"Maybe I should just do it all myself."
But here’s the truth:
- No one will care like you do.
- No one will match your standards immediately.
You don’t find the perfect team.
You build one.
So, I tried again.
I hired. I trained. I refined my process.
I taught them how to think like me.
How to care like I do.
And that changed everything.
One great hire became two.
Two became a team.
Now, we don’t just complete projects.
We fully transform brands.
If you’re a freelancer feeling stuck
Take the leap. Hire someone.
Trust me, it’s the best decision you’ll make.
