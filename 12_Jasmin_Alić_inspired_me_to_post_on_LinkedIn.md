Jasmin Alić inspired me to post on LinkedIn
and now I have 28,000 followers
I think I didn't thank him enough.
Three years ago my Instagram got hacked.
10,000 followers and my only source of clients, gone in one single night.
At that time I had 2 retainer clients left. That was it.
One of them was selling a course. 
Me and my team were the ones behind him, 
writing and designing his content and course material.
I started using LinkedIn just to check how his posts were performing so I could make them better.
That's the only reason I was even on the platform.
Just lurking in the background, doing my job.
Then I kept seeing this one guy pop up in my feed.
Same message. Every few days. Over and over.
Just start posting on LinkedIn.
I saw it maybe ten times in two months. 
I thought, if he keeps saying the same thing this consistently, 
there has to be something to it.
So I sat down one night and actually posted. 
Today we have 28,000 followers on LinkedIn.
A fully booked agency with 3 full time designers.
And a coaching business I'm building for financial advisors.
All of it traces back to one guy who had no idea I existed.
Jasmin was just showing up, 
saying what he believed, 
repeating it until it landed with someone.
It landed with me.
So here's me doing the same for you.
If you've been sitting on the edge of posting on LinkedIn, this is your sign.
You don't need a perfect strategy.
You don't need a big audience to start.
You just need to start.
You have no idea who's watching.
P.S. If you are watching this Jasmin Alić, thank you. I had a 1:1 conversation with you once. I don't know if you remember me. But you got me started, and I won't forget that.
