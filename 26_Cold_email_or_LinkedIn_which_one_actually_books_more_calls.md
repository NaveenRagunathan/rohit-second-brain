Cold email or LinkedIn → which one actually books more calls?
I’ve sent thousands of cold emails.
And I’ve sent hundreds of LinkedIn DMs.
Both work.
But in very different ways.
Cold email:
- Scales like crazy.
- You can reach almost anyone.
- But… reply rates are low (1–2%).
- Deliverability is a constant headache.
- Feels more transactional.
LinkedIn:
- Lower volume (you can’t blast thousands).
- But reply rates are much higher (10%+ easy).
- People see your profile, posts, and brand → trust is built before the pitch.
Conversations start more naturally.
That’s the trade-off.
Email = reach.
→ LinkedIn = trust.
The best setup? Use both.
Email gets you in front of more people.
LinkedIn warms them up and turns replies into relationships.
If you’re only choosing one, think about:
1. Your market size
2. Where your buyers actually spend time
3. Whether you want volume (email) or connection (LinkedIn)
For my agency ContentRizz, the sweet spot has been blending them.
Email opens the door. 
LinkedIn closes it.
