I bought a house last year.
My mum still asks when I'm getting a job.
She doesn't understand what I do.
She just sees money coming and going with no company, no boss to call if something goes wrong.
Mostly I'm either with friends or in my room working on a laptop.
Where we grew up, that's not success.
That's suspicious.
Where I come from, if a young guy is bringing in this kind of money with no job title, no office, no boss, there's only one explanation people jump to.
I've tried explaining it. Many times.
Content design. Coaching. Clients. Projects. Remote work.
Her eyes glaze over around the third sentence.
And honestly? I get it. 
She grew up in a world where safety meant a salary slip and a boss you could call. 
What I do doesn't fit that world. It probably never will.
So I've stopped trying to explain.
She'll see the house. 
She'll see I'm okay. 
That has to be enough.
If you're building something online that the people around you still don't understand, drop it in the comments. 
You're not alone.
