Independent Financial Advisors
This is how inbound works on LinkedIn.
Step 01 - Connect
Connect with 20 ideal clients every day.
If your ideal client is a 45+ year old tech executive, 
Find where they hang out and send them a connect request. 
Make sure your profile is well optimised with your offer.
Step 02 - Sales content
Once they're in your network and your profile is sharp, 
they'll usually know what you do.
Your job now is to make them act on it.
Informative posts and stories get likes. 
They won't get your ideal client DMing you. 
You need to post commercially, one piece of sales content per week that puts your offer in front of them clearly.
Step 03 - Collect and close
Some people will like your posts, 
check your profile, 
and never message you.
Go to them. 
DM anyone who engages but doesn't reach out. 
Short, direct, no pitch. Just open a conversation.
And when people do DM you from your sales content, 
have your next steps ready so you're not fumbling the close.
That's the whole system.
- Clear offer. 
- Clear profile. 
- Clear ideal client. 
- Clear process to onboard and deliver.
If your LinkedIn isn't generating conversations right now, DM me the last three posts you published. 
I'll tell you exactly what's missing.
