**Date:** 10m

This ONE obsession wasted months of my life, 
and landed me zero clients. 👇
Back then, I thought if I could just perfect my portfolio,
the clients would magically show up.
So I spent months in Figma:
→ Tuning layouts
→ Tweaking colors
→ Rewriting case studies
→ Obsessing over animations no client cared about
It felt super productive. 
But it was just a distraction.
- No calls.
- No leads.
-No growth.
I told myself I was “getting ready.”
But truthfully?
I was hiding.
→ Hiding from selling.
→ Hiding from rejection.
→ Hiding from conversations that felt scary.
I built the perfect portfolio...
for other designers to admire.
But the people who actually pay?
They never saw it.
They didn’t care.
Fast forward to now:
My new site is still not live.
But we’re booked solid.
Because I finally stopped tweaking 
and started talking to real people.
→ I sent messages.
→ I pitched offers.
→ I focused on outcomes, not pixels.
And it worked.
Because clients don’t pay for “cool.”
They pay for results.
📌 Reminder:
If you work hard on the wrong things,
you’ll never get ahead.
Stop hiding behind your portfolio.
Start talking to people who can pay you.
