“Everyone blooms on their own timeline.”
 That’s the worst advice you can give to someone ambitious.
Because it sounds comforting…
but it kills urgency.
Yes, we all grow at different paces 
but using that as an excuse to stay stuck is how people waste years.
If you want to bloom faster:
→ Track your growth everyday (self-reflect).
→ Learn from people already where you want to be.
→ Set uncomfortable deadlines (all the time).
→ Don’t hide behind “my timeline.” (bullshit).
You’re not a flower. 💯
